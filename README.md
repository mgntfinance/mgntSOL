## 🌊 What Is mgntSOL?

mgntSOL is a liquid staking token issued by Magnet Finance via Sanctum. When you stake SOL through Magnet Finance, you receive mgntSOL — a fully transferable, liquid representation of your staked SOL position.

**The core value proposition:**

- Earn SOL staking yield automatically as mgntSOL appreciates
- Remain fully liquid — trade, swap, or provide liquidity with your mgntSOL at any time
- Earn Season 1 Genesis Points passively just by holding
- No lock-up period — unstake at any time

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

```
Stake SOL → Receive mgntSOL → Earn Staking Yield + Genesis Points → Stay Liquid
```

1. Stake SOL at [app.sanctum.so/stake/mgntSOL](https://app.sanctum.so/stake/mgntSOL)
2. Receive mgntSOL representing your staked position
3. mgntSOL appreciates in value relative to SOL as staking rewards accrue
4. Hold mgntSOL to earn Genesis Points (updated every 5 minutes)
5. Swap or unstake at any time via the Meteora DLMM pool
